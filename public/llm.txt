# Asay Global - Structural Solutions

## Overview
Asay Global is a premier B2B partner specializing in the supply and distribution of high-quality structural solutions. We provide a wide range of architectural products including u-PVC profiles, aluminum systems, glass railings, and steel reinforcement solutions. We are dedicated to ensuring safety, durability, and aesthetic excellence in every project.

## Product Categories & Services

### Handrails & Railing Systems
- **Railing Systems**: Comprehensive range including RS10 round profile systems, square profiles, and custom designs.
- **Glass Railing Systems**: Modern, frameless, and semi-framed glass railings for balconies, terraces, and stairways.
- **Fence Systems**: Durable aluminum and glass fence solutions for perimeter safety.
- **Glass Holders**: High-quality stainless steel and aluminum glass holders and spider systems.

### Aluminum Systems
- **Door & Window Systems**: Thermal and non-thermal aluminum profiles.
- **Facade Systems**: Curtain walls and architectural cladding solutions.
- **Office Partition Systems**: Sleek glass and aluminum partitions for commercial spaces.

### u-PVC Systems
- **Window & Door Profiles**: Energy-efficient and durable u-PVC systems using Penwood and other premium brands.
- **Roller Shutter Systems**: Integrated shutter solutions for security and light control.

### Glass Solutions
- **Tempered & Laminated Glass**: Safety glass for architectural applications.
- **Balcony Glazing**: Folding and sliding glass systems for versatile living spaces.

## Technical Info
- We serve B2B clients, architects, and construction companies.
- Products are tested for structural safety and compliance with international standards.
